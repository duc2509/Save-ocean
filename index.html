Save The Ocean With Us

❝ Plastics are the most common element found in the ocean today. Plastic, in particular, is harmful to the environment as it does not break down easily and is often mistaken as food by marine animals.

❝ Plastics cause more than 80% of the negative effects on animals associated with ocean trash.

❝ There are 25 trillion pieces of plastic debris in the ocean. Of that, 269,000 tons float on the surface, while some four billion plastic microfibers per square kilometer litter the deep sea.

Our Mission

We plant coral

We have planted more than 10000 corals at all our locations around the wolrd and build habitat for over 500 species. 1 coral for every $5 donation.

We collect trash

Thousands kilograms of trash are collected each year by our volunteers and 60% of them are recycled to become beautyful hand-made decoration.

We educate people

We have almost 100 classes and 300 teachers who will spread ocean knowledge to all local people and traveller. All are free !

Volunteer with our Crew

All One Ocean is run by volunteers who are passionate about the health of our Ocean and we can always use your help! ​Whether it is for 1 hour or 20 we will find a place for you. We provide training and help wherever you need it!
